The Cuban Freedom Flights
A Historical Mixed Reality Experience


About the Project

The Cuban Freedom Flights project invites you to embark on a journey through time, exploring a pivotal chapter in Cuban-American history. Through the immersive power of Mixed Reality, we bring to life the remarkable stories and events surrounding the "Los vuelos de la libertad" – the Freedom Flights.

What to Expect


Captivating Galleries

Immerse yourself in stunning galleries featuring authentic photographs from the era. Our Mixed Reality technology transforms these images, giving them depth and scale to create a truly immersive experience.

Interactive History

Dive into the rich history of the Cuban Freedom Flights as you navigate along a timeline, uncovering the significance of this monumental operation and its enduring impact on American society.

Personal Stories

Hear the voices of those who lived through the Cuban Freedom Flights. Through a blend of animation, physical models, and scanned artifacts, their stories come to life in vivid detail.

Why Choose Us:
  • Authenticity: We are dedicated to preserving the authenticity of this historical narrative while leveraging cutting-edge technology to make it accessible and engaging to audiences of all ages.
  • Education and Awareness: Our project aims to educate and raise awareness about this important chapter in Cuban-American relations, fostering a deeper understanding of the human stories behind the headlines.
  • Innovation: By harnessing the power of Mixed Reality, we push the boundaries of storytelling, offering a unique and unforgettable experience that transcends traditional media.
Get Involved:

Join us on this extraordinary journey through history. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a technology aficionado, or simply curious to learn more, the Cuban Freedom Flights Experience welcomes you to explore, engage, and discover the past in a whole new way.
